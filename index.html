Ifficienato - Proprietary Lead Management Solution

Cloud-Based. Powerful. Simple. Affordable.

Ifficient’s lead-generation platform, Ifficienato, was architected and developed by a small group of individuals with more than 30 years of lead-generation experience in multiple verticals to provide our clients comprehensive lead-management solutions with an extremely simple interface. Because we utilize a cloud-based platform, the only requirement for clients is a supported browser on any operating system!

Ifficienato comes with an infrastructure supporting a fully redundant and scalable solution that is capable of handling millions of transactions per day while staying available in a premier SSAE-16-, HIPAA-, and PCI-compliant data center.

Either take the keys and drive with our self-service option or let The Education Project (TEP) operations team oversee and coordinate your lead-generation initiatives through our full-service option. Either option provides you with full platform capabilities so you can set out to accomplish the important challenges that follow.
